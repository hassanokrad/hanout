🏪 Hanout

A simple, offline manager for your shop — sales, stock, the credit book, and reports. مرحباً 👋

What it is

Hanout is a single file that runs in any web browser — on a phone, a tablet, or a computer. It needs no internet and no account. Everything you record is saved on your own device. Open the file once, and start tapping.

Install

On a computer: just double-click hanout.html to open it in your browser. Bookmark it.

On a phone: open the file in your browser, then use “Add to Home screen”. It will get its own icon and open full-screen, like a normal app.

Because it lives on the device, each phone/computer keeps its own data. Use Backup to move data between devices.

The tabs

🛒
SellTap a product, choose the price and quantity, and pick Cash or Credit. See today’s total and undo a mistake with one tap.
📦
InventoryYour products and how many are left. Stock goes down automatically with each sale, and items running low are flagged.
📒
Debts (كريدي)Who owes you money. A credit sale is added here automatically; record repayments as customers pay you back.
💸
ExpensesWhat you spend — restocking, rent, electricity. This makes your profit in Reports real, not just sales.
👥
ContactsCustomers and suppliers, each with their purchase history and what they owe.
📊
ReportsRevenue, profit, best-sellers and trends — for this month, last month, or all time.
⚙️
SettingsYour shop name, currency, language (English / Français / العربية), light or dark theme, and which tabs you want to show.
💾
BackupSave all your data to a file, restore it, or export your sales to a spreadsheet (CSV).

Back up your data important

Your data lives only on this device. From time to time, open Backup → Export backup and keep the file somewhere safe (email it to yourself, save it to the cloud, or a USB stick). To move to a new phone, install Hanout there and use Import backup.

Make it yours

In Settings you can rename the business, change the currency symbol, switch language, and turn modules on or off — so a café, a boutique, or a corner shop each see only what they need. Hanout is free and open-source (MIT). Developers can add their own modules — see the project README.md.
